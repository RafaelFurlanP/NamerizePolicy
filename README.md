    <h1>Privacy Policy for Namerize</h1>
    <p class="update-date">Last Updated: January 2025</p>

    <h2>1. Introduction</h2>
    <p>Welcome to Namerize ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our contact management application.</p>

    <h2>2. Information We Collect</h2>
    
    <h3>2.1 Information You Provide</h3>
    <p>When you use Namerize, you may provide the following information:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Contact details (names, how you met them)</li>
        <li>Meeting information (date, location)</li>
        <li>Physical descriptions (gender, age, hair color, height)</li>
        <li>Photos of contacts</li>
        <li>Social connections between contacts</li>
        <li>Review preferences and reminder settings</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>2.2 Automatically Collected Information</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Location data (GPS coordinates) when you mark where you met someone</li>
        <li>App usage preferences (theme, language, notification settings)</li>
        <li>Device permissions status</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>3. How We Use Your Information</h2>
    <p>We use your information to:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Store and manage your contacts locally on your device</li>
        <li>Send you reminder notifications to review contacts</li>
        <li>Display meeting locations on maps</li>
        <li>Enable data export and import functionality</li>
        <li>Manage subscription status (for premium features)</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>4. Data Storage and Security</h2>
    <p><strong>Local Storage:</strong> All your contact data is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. We do not upload, sync, or backup your personal data to any cloud servers.</p>
    <p><strong>Photos:</strong> Contact photos are compressed and stored in your device's private app directory.</p>
    <p><strong>Security:</strong> Your data is protected by your device's security measures. We recommend using device-level security features like passcodes or biometric authentication.</p>

    <h2>5. Third-Party Services</h2>
    <p>Namerize uses the following third-party services:</p>
    
    <h3>5.1 RevenueCat</h3>
    <p>We use RevenueCat to manage premium subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Purchase information</li>
        <li>Device identifiers</li>
        <li>Subscription status</li>
    </ul>
    <p>View RevenueCat's privacy policy at: <a href="https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy">https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy</a></p>

    <h3>5.2 Google Maps</h3>
    <p>We use Google Maps to display meeting locations. Google Maps may collect:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Location data</li>
        <li>Map interaction data</li>
    </ul>
    <p>View Google's privacy policy at: <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy">https://policies.google.com/privacy</a></p>

    <h2>6. Device Permissions</h2>
    <p>Namerize requests the following permissions:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Location:</strong> To mark where you met contacts</li>
        <li><strong>Camera:</strong> To take photos of contacts</li>
        <li><strong>Storage:</strong> To save contact photos and export/import data</li>
        <li><strong>Notifications:</strong> To send review reminders</li>
    </ul>
    <p>You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.</p>

    <h2>7. Data Sharing</h2>
    <p>We do NOT:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Sell your personal information</li>
        <li>Share your contact data with third parties</li>
        <li>Upload your data to cloud servers</li>
        <li>Use your data for advertising</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>8. Your Rights and Choices</h2>
    <p>You have complete control over your data:</p>
    <ul>

        <li><strong>Export:</strong> Export your contacts to JSON format</li>
        <li><strong>Delete:</strong> Remove individual contacts or all data</li>
        <li><strong>Portability:</strong> Import/export functionality for data transfer</li>
